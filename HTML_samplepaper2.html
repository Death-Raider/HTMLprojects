SWINE FLU PANDEMIC

H1N1 influenza is a virus that causes illness in people and spreads from one person to another in the same way as the common flu. Detected first in April 2009 in mexico, the disease soon spread across different countries in the world and was declared the swine flu pendemic by the World Health Organization in june 2009.
